Attendance

Most Simple yet powerful Attendance App for Schools and Colleges.

Quick Setup :

  1. Add your own divisions.
  2. Delete unwanted divisions.
  3. Set Your Preferences

Using Attendnace :

  1. Select your division by Arrows.
  2. Tap Floating Action Button (FAB).
  3. Button will turn RED
  4. Tap Absent numbers.
  5. Tap FAB again to finish and Save
  6. Do the same for next division.
